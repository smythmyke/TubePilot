TubePilot

AI-powered YouTube metadata generator. SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and tags — right inside YouTube Studio.

Features

AI Metadata Generation

Generate SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and categories powered by AI. Tailored to your channel and audience.

YouTube Studio Integration

Works directly inside YouTube Studio via a slide-out panel. One click to fill your video metadata — no tab switching.

Channel-Aware Context

Reads your channel name, keywords, description, and audience to generate metadata that matches your brand voice.

Upload Automation

Select a video file, generate metadata, and upload — TubePilot fills the details, sets visibility, and publishes for you.

Product Integration

Add products with SEO keywords. TubePilot weaves natural mentions into your descriptions for affiliate and sponsored content.

Playlist Management

Select a playlist before uploading. TubePilot automatically adds your video to the chosen playlist after publishing.

How It Works

  1. Install the TubePilot Chrome extension
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Connect YouTube when prompted — only requested when you use YouTube features
  4. Describe your video in the TubePilot panel on any YouTube Studio page
  5. Generate and apply — review the AI-generated metadata, then fill the form or upload with one click

YouTube Data Access

TubePilot uses the YouTube Data API v3 to read and update your video metadata, channel information, and playlists. You remain in full control — TubePilot never publishes or modifies content without your explicit action. All YouTube API calls go directly from the extension to Google's servers.
