TubePilot

AI-powered YouTube metadata generator. Create SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and tags for your videos — right inside YouTube Studio.

What TubePilot Does

  • Generates optimized titles, descriptions, and tags using AI
  • Works directly inside YouTube Studio — no tab switching
  • Reads your channel context (name, keywords, audience) for tailored results
  • Fills metadata into YouTube Studio forms with one click
  • Manages playlists and upload settings
  • Supports product integration for affiliate and sponsored content

How It Works

  • Install the Chrome extension
  • Sign in with your Google account
  • Grant YouTube access when prompted (only when you use YouTube features)
  • Open YouTube Studio and describe your video
  • TubePilot generates metadata and fills the form for you

YouTube Data Access

TubePilot uses the YouTube Data API v3 to read and update your video metadata, channel information, and playlists. You remain in full control — TubePilot never publishes or modifies content without your explicit action. See our Privacy Policy for full details on data handling.

Get Started

TubePilot is available on the Chrome Web Store.
